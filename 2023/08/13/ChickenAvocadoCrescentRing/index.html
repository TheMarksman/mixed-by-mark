Chicken Avocado Crescent Ring

Yield: 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 oz) can crescent rolls
  • ‚Öî c shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 c cooked, diced chicken
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • ¬º c bacon bits
  • 1 egg
  • Sea salt for sprinkling on top
  • ¬Ω c Ranch
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp minced onion

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place an oven-safe bowl in the middle of the baking sheet. Unroll the crescent roll dough, placing the triangles to overlap each other w/ the bowl in the center.
  3. Sprinkle half the cheese around the base of the crescent ring.
  4. Place the chicken & avocado over the cheese.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese & bacon bits on top of the chicken.
  6. Roll the narrow end of each triangle over, tucking the end underneath.
  7. Beat the egg. Brush egg over the top of the crescent ring. Sprinkle w/ sea salt.
  8. Bake 15-20 minutes, until golden brown.
  9. While the ring is baking, prepare the dipping sauce. Combine ranch, basil, garlic powder, & minced onion in a bowl, & mix well.
  10. Once ring is baked, allow to cool for a couple minutes. Then transfer to a serving platter, placing the dipping sauce in the middle.
