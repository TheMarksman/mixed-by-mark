Pretzel Treat Cookies

Yield: 40 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter, softened
  • ¬Ω c butter shortening
  • ¬Ω c sugar
  • 1 c brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2¬º c all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¬Ω c Hershey's Hugs, roughly chopped
  • ¬Ω c pretzels, broken into pieces
  • ¬Ω c M&M's

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F. Grease a baking sheet.
  2. Using a stand mixer, cream together butter & butter shortening.
  3. Add sugars & vanilla to butter mixture, and mix until smooth.
  4. Add eggs to the mixture; mix until combined.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, & salt.
  6. Slowly add the flour mixture to the dough, mixing between each addition.
  7. Fold in the chopped Hershey's Hugs, broken pretzels, & M&M's by hand.
  8. Scoop the dough into 1-inch balls on prepared baking sheet.
  9. Bake 8-10 minutes, until the cookies are golden brown.
