Cinnamon Cookies

Yield: 2 dozen cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 c all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ¬æ c butter, melted
  • 1 c sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325¬∞F. Grease a baking sheet.
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer, combine the sugar and melted butter.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla to the sugar mixture. Mix til combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, cinnamon, & baking powder.
  5. Slowly add the flour mixture to the sugar mixture, mixing between each addition.
  6. Place the dough in the fridge to chill for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Once the dough is chilled, roll out the dough into 1-inch balls, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 9 minutes, until the cookies are just slightly set.
  9. Remove the cookies from the oven. Use a spoon to gently press the middle of the cookies to get their sunken-in shape.
  10. Allow the cookies to cool some before removing from the pan.
